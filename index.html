I am a master student (thesis-based) in David R. Cheriton School of Computer Science at University of Waterloo, supervised by Prof. Olga Vechtomova and Prof. Lili Mou. I will join UCL NLP in Fall 2020 as a PhD student, supervised by Prof. Pontus Stenetorp and Prof. Sebastian Riedel. Previously, I was affiliated with Montreal Institute of Learning Algorithm (MILA), and Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (AMII). I got my bachelor degree from Tongji University, Shanghai, China.

I work closely with researchers from several institutes, including Prof. Laurent Charlin from MILA, Prof. Randy Goebel from University of Alberta, Prof. Tianwei Yu from Emory University, Prof. Min Yang from Chinese Academy of Sciences and Prof. Ye Luo from Tongji University.

My primary research interest is about Natural Language Processing (NLP).
