I am a PhD student at UCL NLP, co-supervised by Prof. Pontus Stenetorp and Prof. Sebastian Riedel. I was affiliated with Montreal Institute of Learning Algorithm (MILA), and Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (AMII).

My primary research interest is Natural Language Processing (NLP).

I will join the UCL Computer Science department as an Assistant Professor in Fall 2025 and co-lead the UCL NLP group.
